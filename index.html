About Me

I’m a quantitative ecologist who has coupled field work with a range of methods, notably individual-based models, to explore questions on foraging behaviour, conservation efforts and life history across the animal kingdom. I’m also interested in scaling relationships, palaeobiology and science communication. I'm in the third year of a PhD at Trinity College Dublin.
